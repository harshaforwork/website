About me

My journey in visual storytelling began at 12 as a hobbyist photographer, quickly growing into a passion for creating narratives through moving images. Over the years, I’ve worked as a freelance cinematographer for YouTube channels, an assistant director on film sets, and a video editor for projects spanning real estate videos to short-form content. Now back to freelance video editing, I’m committed to refining my craft and ultimately aim to create films that deeply resonate with audiences. Let’s connect and explore opportunities to bring powerful stories to life!
